The Social Calendar

Have you ever tried to find a time when 3 grown adults are all free? As we get older with new responsibilities of new partners, spouses, in-laws, maybe even a child, it’s a miracle if we found an hour of free time within a month’s time frame.

Using The Social Calendar, friends can express their preferred times/dates for upcoming events on a real-time calendar. So there’s a clear way to visualize and see which times work best for a majority of the group.

To Do List

Passive-Agressive Sticky Notes: move over. There’s finally a way to remind your roommates/spouse/ of the task they agreed to a week ago, without seeming pushy or overbearing. This to do list not only lets all members easily see what needs to be accomplished, but sends the responsible party text message reminders.

According to my husband, a frequent receiver of these reminders, they work great because the texts don’t come with an eye roll and a cute, but condescending tone.

Trainer Bot

A free personal trainer? Not exactly. It won’t know if you quit halfway through your workout and started eating cheetos, but it does help you plan out your workout session and coaches you through your workout. No need to time yourself or think of what to do next, so all you have to do is focus on yourself.
